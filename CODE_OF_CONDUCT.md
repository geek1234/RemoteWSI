# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas in good faith.
- Give actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or hostile behavior.
- Sharing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct and may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate these rules.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private security/reporting channel with maintainers (see `SECURITY.md`).
